# Screening disclaimer

**Source id:** `agent-disclaimer`

This assistant is a **proof-of-concept informal screening tool** for North Carolina Food and Nutrition Services (FNS / SNAP).

- It is **not** an official determination.
- Only your **county Department of Social Services (DSS)** can approve or deny benefits.
- Rules change; this POC uses a **versioned, dated** public ruleset and curated documents.
- Do **not** share Social Security numbers, full street addresses, or other sensitive identifiers in this chat.
- If you are in crisis, contact emergency services or appropriate crisis hotlines; this tool cannot provide emergency response.

For official next steps, use public channels such as NC ePASS (https://epass.nc.gov/) or your local county DSS.

## Apply options (public information only)

Residents can typically:

- Apply online through **NC ePASS**: https://epass.nc.gov/
- Contact or visit their **local county Department of Social Services (DSS)** for paper applications, interviews, and case processing

## What you may need

Applications often require documentation such as:

- Proof of identity
- Proof of income
- Proof of residency / household information
- Student status documents (if applicable)

Exact document lists vary by case.

## What this agent will never do

- Submit an application on your behalf
- Contact DSS or any agency for you
- Scrape or log into government systems
- Make an official eligibility determination

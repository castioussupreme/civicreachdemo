# CalFresh screening disclaimer

**Source id:** `calfresh-disclaimer`

This assistant is a **proof-of-concept informal screening tool** for California CalFresh (SNAP).

- It is **not** an official determination.
- Only your **county human services agency** can approve or deny benefits.
- Rules change; this POC uses a **versioned, dated** public ruleset and curated documents.
- Do **not** share Social Security numbers, full street addresses, or other sensitive identifiers in this chat.

For official next steps, use public channels such as BenefitsCal (https://www.benefitscal.com/) or your county office.

# How to apply for CalFresh (public pointers)

**Source id:** `calfresh-how-to-apply`
**Public source:** CDSS CalFresh
**URL:** https://www.cdss.ca.gov/calfresh

## Apply options (public information only)

Residents can typically:

- Apply online through **BenefitsCal**: https://www.benefitscal.com/
- Contact their **county human services / social services agency** for paper applications, interviews, and case processing

## What this agent will never do

- Submit an application on your behalf
- Contact a county agency for you
- Scrape or log into government systems
- Make an official eligibility determination

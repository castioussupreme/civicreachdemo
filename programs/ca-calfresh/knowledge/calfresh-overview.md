# CalFresh program overview

**Source id:** `calfresh-overview`
**Public source:** California Department of Social Services – CalFresh
**URL:** https://www.cdss.ca.gov/calfresh

CalFresh is California’s name for the federal **SNAP** (food assistance) program. It helps low-income individuals and families buy food.

Unique marker for isolation tests: **CALFRESH_MARKER**.

## High-level factors that affect eligibility

Public materials state that CalFresh eligibility depends on factors including:

- Income and household size
- Residency in California
- Resources / assets (not fully modeled in this screening POC)
- Student rules, work requirements, and immigration status in some cases (not fully modeled here)

**This agent performs an informal likelihood screen only.** Only a county human services agency can make an official determination.

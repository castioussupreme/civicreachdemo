# NC SNAP/FNS gross monthly income limits (FFY 2025 / Oct 2024–Sep 2025)

**Source id:** `nc-fns-income-limits-2024`
**Public basis:** USDA SNAP COLA / federal poverty guidelines (200% of FPL, 48 contiguous states), used by NC FNS public screening tables for that year
**URL:** https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/allotment/COLA
**Also see:** https://morefood.org/using-snap/am-i-eligible/
**Effective:** October 1, 2024 – September 30, 2025
**Ruleset id used by this POC:** `nc-fns-screening-2024-10`

> **Dual copy:** Same table as `programs/nc-fns/rules/2024-10.yaml`. Prior-year
> version retained for multi-version / effective-period resolution. See `AGENTS.md`.

## Important caveats

- Public **Maximum Gross Monthly Income (200%)** style table for FFY 2025.
- Some households face a stricter (~130%) test; DSS decides (see `nc-fns-gross-income-tests`).
- Superseded on Oct 1, 2025 by the FFY 2026 table (`nc-fns-income-limits` / ruleset `2025-10`).
